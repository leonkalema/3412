RWB Comprehensive Movement Guide

Attacking Movements:

  • Overlap Run (Yellow): Sprint forward beyond the wide midfielder to receive passes in advanced areas. Create crossing opportunities from the byline.
  • Inside Channel Run (Orange): Make diagonal runs into the half-spaces between opposition defense and midfield, creating passing lanes and attacking options.
  • Wide Support Position (Gold): Hold width to stretch opposition defense while maintaining possession, providing passing options and recycling play when needed.

Defensive Movements:

  • Defensive Recovery (Red): Quickly track back to form part of a back-5 when possession is lost, closing spaces behind and protecting the defensive third.
  • Central Coverage (Light Red): Shift inward to provide defensive cover when ball is on the opposite side, maintaining team shape and preventing central penetration.
  • Wide Pressing (Dark Red): Apply pressure to opposition wide players when they receive the ball, forcing play inside toward your defensive midfielders or forcing errors.

Transition Responsibilities:

  • Defense to Attack: Be ready to quickly advance forward when possession is won, providing immediate width and attacking outlet.
  • Attack to Defense: React immediately when possession is lost, choose between counter-pressing or recovering defensive position based on team strategy.
